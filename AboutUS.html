Ashvina Shah

Age: 26

I founded the organization Roar for Rescue in March of 2022, after a trip to the northern India. During the trip, I become aware of the practices of hunting, poaching and capturing of tigers by local communties. This injustice instigated me to find a way to stop this harsh treatement. After establishing this non-profit and gathering enough support, the Roar for Rescue team was able to pass a bill which made hunting, poaching and capturing Tigers for any reason illegal and punishable by fines and jail time. Afterward, we expanded our goal to be worldwide rather than interregional. Our mission is to remove the Bengal Tigers from the IUCN Red List of Threatened Species and the WWF list of Endangered Species, through a combition of laws, volunteer and restoration program, and raising awareness of this issue.
