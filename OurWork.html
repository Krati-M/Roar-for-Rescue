Our Achievements:









1st Bill: Life without Fear

The reason that I started this non-porfit was to raise awareness of the hunting, capturing and illegal poaching of bengal tigers occuring in Northern India, and find support to have my legistation pass to have stricter policing and laws against this. In September 2022, this bill become a reality and since then, we have seen a 40% decrease in poaching occuring in those states.

Laws Passed

Bill in Kerla, India: Made Mangrove Forests in Northern Kerala a national protected area

Bill in Goa, India: Created two national park sactunary and established a research center for care and surveillance of Bengal Tigers in Southern India
Passed in December 2022
